ABOUT

Hungry after a long evening of studying? Come to Bits n' Bytes, where our meals are prepared using the finest and freshest ingredients. We have a selection of drinks and excellent food ranging from healthy salads to delicious main dishes. Whether you need something quick or healthy including a vegetarian and gluten free choices plus affordable,this is the place to be. We are around the corner in the T-Building at the Algonquin College, come on in!

Breakfast

Try our daily Breakfast. Scramble with squash, zucchini, sundried tomatoes and spinach. Served with sweet potato hash and toast along with our own Gourmet coffee.

Lunch

Our lunches are all freshly made. Try our mediterranean wrap with house made hummus, organic romaine lettuce, carrot, feta, quinoa, tabbouleh, roasted red pepper kalamata olives. Your choice of Gluten free or vegan.

Salads

We have a wide selection of our very own salads. Try our Grilled Veggie Salad with fried greens, grilled portobello mushrooms, roasted red peoppers, red onions and balsamic vinaigrette dressing.

Vegan

We have a variety of dishes in Vegan style. Enjoy our Black Bean Burger. A homemade blackbean quinoa burger served with advocado, lettuce, tomato, onion and topped witha roasted pepper aioli. Served on a bed of spinach.
