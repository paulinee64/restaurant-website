ABOUT

Hungry after a long evening of studying? Come to Bits n' Bytes, where our meals are prepared using the finest and freshest ingredients. We have a selection of drinks and excellent food ranging from healthy salads to delicious main dishes. Whether you need something quick or healthy including a vegetarian and gluten free choices plus affordable, this is the place to be. We are around the corner in the T-Building at the Algonquin College, come on in!
